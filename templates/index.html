Understanding Pneumonia

Pneumonia is an infection that inflames the air sacs in one or both lungs, which may fill with fluid or pus, causing symptoms like cough, fever, chills, and difficulty breathing.

Common Causes:

  • Bacteria (e.g., Streptococcus pneumoniae)
  • Viruses (e.g., Influenza)
  • Fungi (in immunocompromised patients)
  • Aspiration (inhaling food or liquids)

Treatments:

  • Antibiotics for bacterial pneumonia
  • Antiviral medications for viral pneumonia
  • Antifungal medications for fungal pneumonia

How Medscan AI Detects Pneumonia

Understanding Brain Tumors

Brain tumors are abnormal growths of cells in the brain, which can be benign or malignant. They can cause symptoms like headaches, seizures, and cognitive changes.

Common Types:

  • Gliomas (e.g., Astrocytoma, Glioblastoma)
  • Meningiomas (tumors in the meninges)
  • Pituitary tumors (affecting the pituitary gland)

Treatments:

  • Surgery to remove the tumor
  • Radiation therapy to shrink the tumor
  • Chemotherapy to target cancer cells

How Medscan AI Detects Brain Tumors

Our AI analyzes MRI scans to detect brain tumors with high precision. It identifies tumor location, size, and type, providing detailed insights for treatment planning.
